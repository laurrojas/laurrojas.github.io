Low Hanging Fruit

As part of my work with the OCAD U Student Press, I designed the pamphlets and promotional material for a series of student gallery shows including Low Hanging Fruit (The Hungy Lion Throws Itself on the Antelope), which showed the work of Emily Grace Harrison, Courtney Gillatly, Cristine Yunyk, and Owen Marshall.

The work in this show was colourful and fun, and I wanted the promotional material to reflect that. By choosing a bright colour palette and printing everything on the risograph (my favourite!), I emphasized the show's bold and playful character. The smiley faces became icons and graphic devices that could translate to print, web, and animated materials.
