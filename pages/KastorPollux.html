Kastor & Pollux Mailer

Kastor & Pollux–a multidisciplinary, creative agency based in Toronto–asked me to design a themed mailer package that they could send to their clients and collaborators as a thank-you for a successful year. This concept revolved around the theme of fruity and fun, highlighting their fresh approach to creative ideas and the variety of their studio work. Under the guidance of K&P’s creative director Dani Roche, I conceptualized, designed, and produced this project, while she designed the fruit stickers. We then packaged 50 of these up in marigold envelopes and into the mail they went.

The mailer package consisted of a poster (printed on the risograph at Vide Press), three postcards outlining some recent projects, a notecard where we hand-wrote a custom message, and a sticker sheet.
